Global aspect_ratio

boost::svg::aspect_ratio — aspect_ratio is a guess at average height to width of font (0.55 to 0.7?). used to estimate the svg length of a title or header string from the font size (height and width of EM - capital M). This can only be quite approximate as varies on type of font and variants (upper or lower, narrow or bold). and the mix of characters widths (unless monospace font). See https://www.w3.org/TR/SVG/text.html#GlyphsMetricshttps://www.w3.org/TR/SVG/text.html#InterfaceSVGTextContentElement Can also use textLength to coerce into an estimated length.
